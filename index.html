Personal Life

Family :- APJ Abdul kalam came into this world on 15th October 1931, at a time when India was under British occupation. He was born to a Tamil Muslim family in Tamil Nadu. His father was a boat owner while his mother was a housewife. Furthermore, Kalam had five siblings and was the youngest of the lot.

Beliefs :- Dr A.P.J.Kalam belonged to a Muslim family, he had his own views about the religion. In his whole life, he never criticized religious faiths other than Islam. Moreover, he had followed non-Islamic rituals, which is forbidden in Islam. Several Muslim fanatics criticized Dr Kalam for his faith in non-Islamic rituals.
